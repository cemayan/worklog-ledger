Worklog Ledger for Jira

Log work in a fast weekly calendar and turn Jira worklogs into client-ready billing reports.

Add to Chrome — coming soon

Weekly calendar

Your worklogs on a Monday-to-Sunday grid. Add, edit, duplicate and delete entries without leaving the page. Copy last week in one click.

Review before upload

New worklogs are staged locally first. Push them to Jira when you're ready — every failure is visible and retryable, nothing is lost silently.

Billing reports

Pick a date range, map projects to clients, set hourly rates, and export an invoice-ready CSV — hours × rate, entry by entry.

No server, no admin

Runs entirely in your browser against Jira's own REST API. No backend, no data collection, no Marketplace admin approval needed.

How it works

Worklog Ledger uses your existing Jira Cloud browser session (or an API token you provide) to read and write worklogs directly — the same way Jira's own UI does. Settings, client mappings and rates are stored in your browser's extension storage. Nothing touches a third-party server.

Pricing

The calendar is free, forever. Billing reports with rates and CSV export are part of the paid plan after a 14-day full-featured trial. Only the person running the reports pays — not every Jira user on your site.
